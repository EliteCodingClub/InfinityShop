Our Story

Infinity Liquor Shop

Located in the heart of Florissant, Missouri, Infinity Liquor Shop has been the community's go-to destination for premium spirits, fine wines, and craft beers. We believe great drinks deserve to be enjoyed in great company — including your home.

Our curated selection spans over 500 products from around the world. Every bottle on our shelves is chosen with care, from aged single malts to vibrant natural wines.
